**Important links useful for GSoC**

- **Official GSoC Homepage** (always the starting point , check for current year announcements): https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/
- **Official Timeline Page** (general timing patterns + current year dates when announced): https://developers.google.com/open-source/gsoc/timeline
- **GSoC Contributor/Student Guide** (MUST-READ — super detailed tips on proposals, community interaction, success): https://google.github.io/gsocguides/student/
- **Past Programs Archive** (browse organizations, projects, and stats from previous years to spot patterns & recurring orgs): https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/archive
- **GSoC Organizations Explorer** : https://www.gsocorganizations.dev/
- **Accepted Proposals Archive** (read real winning proposals for inspiration — **never copy**, just learn structure & depth): https://github.com/Google-Summer-of-Code-Archive/gsoc-proposals-archive (community-maintained, has many years)
- **FAQ Page** (eligibility, rules, common questions): https://developers.google.com/open-source/gsoc/faq
-  **Google Open Source Blog** (news, org announcements, program updates): https://opensource.googleblog.com/

  Feel free to raise a PR if you want to add more links
